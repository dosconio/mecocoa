# Driver

## x86/x64l

- CRT Video (by BIOS)
- Interrupt 8259 **PIC**
- Interrupt 8254 **PIT**
- **RTC**
- **Buzzer** PC Speaker
- **KBD** Keyboard and **MOU** PS/2-Mouse
- IDE Disk

## x64u

- Bus: **PCI**
- LAPIC Timer and ACPI PM Timer
- **KBD** USB-Keyboard and **MOU** USB-Mouse
